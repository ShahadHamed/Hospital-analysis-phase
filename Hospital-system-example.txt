5. COMPLETE HOSPITAL SYSTEM EXAMPLE

Below is a full example similar to your banking sample, but customized for a Hospital Information System.

US-001: Secure Patient Login to Hospital Portal
User Story

As a patient, I want to securely log into my hospital portal using my username and password, so that I can view my medical records and appointments safely.

Acceptance Criteria

Successful Login
• Given I am a registered patient with valid credentials
• When I enter my correct username and password and click “Login”
• Then I am authenticated and redirected to my patient dashboard

Invalid Credentials
• Given I am a registered patient
• When I enter an incorrect username or password
• Then I see an error message “Invalid credentials. Please try again.”

Account Lock for Security
• Account is temporarily locked after 5 consecutive failed attempts
• An email notification is sent to the patient informing them of failed attempts

Password Reset Flow
• Given I am on the login page
• When I click “Forgot Password”
• Then I am redirected to the password reset page

Security Requirements
• Passwords must be encrypted and never displayed
• Session expires after 15 minutes of inactivity
• All login attempts are logged for security audits
• Only verified accounts can access medical data
